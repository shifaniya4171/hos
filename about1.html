About us

healthy guard Hospital is the most accredited hospital in the central province healthcare sector. Since 1997, healthy guard has revolutionized the healthcare industry through infrastructure development and advancement of products and services, with a view to deliver healthcare that is on par with national medical standards.

Chairman's Message

At Healthy Guard Hospital, our core mission is to deliver compassionate, innovative, and exceptional healthcare to all members of our community.
