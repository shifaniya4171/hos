OUR SERVICES

At Healthy Guard, we are dedicated to providing a wide range of services to ensure the best possible experience for our patients. Our Hospital Management System is designed to handle all the important aspects of hospital care, making sure everything runs smoothly. From the moment a patient registers to the time they leave, our system helps make the process easy and efficient. We focus on delivering high-quality care and support through our reliable system, ensuring that both patients and staff have everything they need to make their experience comfortable and hassle-free.


  1. Registering Patients: We make it easy for patients to sign up and get started with their healthcare journey.
  2. Scheduling Appointments: Booking appointments is quick and simple, so patients can see doctors at times that work best for them.
  3. Billing: Our system handles payments and bills efficiently, making it easier for patients to manage their healthcare costs.
  4. Medical Records: We securely store and manage patient records so doctors can access important information when needed.

In addition to these, We also provide:


  1. Parking: Convenient parking spaces for both patients and visitors.
  2. Food Court: A variety of food options are available for patients, staff, and visitors.
  3. Pharmacy: A fully stocked pharmacy where patients can get their prescribed medicines easily.
  4. Outpatient Department (OPD) Services: For patients who don't need to stay overnight, our OPD offers quick and effective treatments.
  5. Laboratory: We have a modern lab for all types of medical tests and diagnostics.
  6. Hospital Rooms: Comfortable rooms for patients who need to stay at the hospital for treatment.
